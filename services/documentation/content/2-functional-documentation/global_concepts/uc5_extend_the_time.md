## Description

A reminder email will be sent before ** expiry** , allowing users to ** extend the retention period**  by clicking the provided link.

---

## Email Functionality

![uc5_email_to_extend.png](../images/uc5_email_to_extend.png)

This email reminds users to extend the data retention period before deletion. It includes a “Renew Data Retention” button, which redirects to the relevant digital service or inventories page.

---

## Digital Service

![uc5_ds_extend.png](../images/uc5_ds_extend.png)

After redirection to the digital service page, a popup titled “Extend the Time Before Data Deletion” is displayed. It includes two buttons: one to extend the retention period (which updates the expiry date) and another to close the popup.
